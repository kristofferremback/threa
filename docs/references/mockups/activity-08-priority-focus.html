Activity 8: Priority Focus

Concept: Summaries ONLY for high-priority items (mentions, AI waiting for you). Everything else is ultra-compact single-line. Priority items get promoted to a special section at the top with mini-cards. The rest of the list stays clean.

Design philosophy:

  • • Needs Attention section surfaces what actually matters
  • • Only 2-3 items max in priority section (AI limits noise)
  • • Regular items are 32px tall - minimal, scannable
  • • Thread weight still visible for ambient activity awareness
  • • No summaries for "just active" channels - only actionable items

When does something become "priority"?

  • • You were @mentioned
  • • AI has something ready for you
  • • Someone asked you a direct question
  • • Time-sensitive request detected
  • • NOT: just because people are chatting
