09e: Interactive Heat Map Bar

Top bar as heat map - hot colors for urgent, cool for quiet. Hover shows label name, click highlights corresponding sidebar section and scrolls to it.

Heat map concept:

  • • Hot (gold) = AI activity
  • • Warm (red) = mentions/urgent
  • • Cool (blue) = people active
  • • Cold (gray) = quiet
  • • Click syncs with sidebar
