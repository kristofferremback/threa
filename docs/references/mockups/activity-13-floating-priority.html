Activity 13: Floating Priority Beacon

Concept: Priority items float as a sticky "beacon" at the top that stays visible as you scroll. The beacon is compact (28px items) and dismissible. The main list stays completely uniform. Items appear in both places - beacon for visibility, list for navigation.

Why floating beacon?

  • • Priority items never scroll out of view
  • • Main list stays uniform and scannable
  • • Beacon is dismissible (hover to see X)
  • • Click beacon to jump to item
  • • Beacon fades to transparent at bottom edge

Try scrolling:

Scroll the sidebar - notice the beacon stays at top while the list scrolls underneath. The gradient fade creates a smooth visual transition.
