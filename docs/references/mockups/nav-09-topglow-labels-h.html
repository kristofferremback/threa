09h: Floating Panels + Top Glow

Ultra-minimal 6px glow bar at top. Each segment is a label. Hover/click reveals a floating panel with that label's streams. Full content area by default. Panel shows grid of items with activity glow.

Concept:

  • • Hover top edge to reveal label panel
  • • Grid layout shows more items at once
  • • Activity glow on per-item basis
  • • Maximum content space (no sidebar)
  • • Panel auto-hides on mouse leave
