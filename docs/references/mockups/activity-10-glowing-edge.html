Activity 10: Glowing Edge

Concept: The ambient edge now glows - the color bleeds softly into the sidebar, creating a warm presence that you notice in your peripheral vision. The 4px bar is the anchor, the glow is the aura.

The glow effect:

  • • Solid 4px edge - the "thread"
  • • 40px gradient glow - bleeds into the row
  • • Color intensity = importance
  • • Quiet items = no glow - just fades away

Why glow works:

  • • Peripheral vision is drawn to light, not shapes
  • • The aura feels alive, not just data
  • • More subtle than badges, more noticeable than just color
  • • Creates "warm" vs "cool" zones naturally

Note:

Toggle dark mode to see how the glow stands out even more against the dark background. The effect is designed to be subtle in light mode, more pronounced in dark.
