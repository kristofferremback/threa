09d: Blurred Edge Collapse

When collapsed, sidebar becomes a blurred, pulsing glow strip. The blur creates a soft, ambient feel. Tiny top bar shows label colors. Click strip to expand.

The blur effect:

  • • Softens the colors for peripheral vision
  • • Gentle pulse animation
  • • Less sharp = less demanding
  • • Hover sharpens slightly as invitation
