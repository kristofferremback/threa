Why this works:

  • • Content-based priority, not count-based
  • • "Bob asked you a question" tells you why to look, not just that you should
  • • AI can help summarize and prioritize what actually matters
  • • Feels conversational, not transactional
  • • Reduces FOMO - you know what you're "missing" and can decide it's OK
