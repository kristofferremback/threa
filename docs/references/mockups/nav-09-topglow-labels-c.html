09c: Segmented Top Bar (No Sidebar)

Labels as horizontal tabs in top bar. Click to expand a panel showing that label's streams. Full-width content area. No sidebar at all.

Concept:

  • • Click labels to see their contents
  • • Glow underline shows activity type
  • • Maximum content area
  • • Works well with few labels (3-5)
