My Recommendations

Based on Threa's positioning as an AI-native knowledge chat for tech professionals.

Top Pick Satoshi

Contemporary and distinctive without being flashy. Has subtle geometric qualities that feel "smart" and technical, but remains warm and readable. The slightly squared terminals give it a modern edge. Free from Fontshare with generous licensing.

Bold Choice Space Grotesk

More obviously technical and futuristic. The geometric shapes and monospace-inspired details scream "tech product." Great if you want Threa to feel cutting-edge and AI-native. Might be polarizing but definitely memorable.

Safe Choice Plus Jakarta Sans

Modern and friendly without being generic. Has enough personality to stand out from system fonts while remaining universally appealing. Good balance of professionalism and approachability. Excellent italic for message emphasis.
