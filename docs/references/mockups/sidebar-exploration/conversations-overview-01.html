Conversations Overview Exploration

Rethinking how streams and conversations are displayed in the sidebar

Current Problems

No Activity Context
Must open each stream to see what's happening. No preview, no timestamp, no author.
Alphabetical Only
Active conversations get buried. No way to sort by "most recent" or "most active".
No Favorites
Can't pin important streams to the top. Must scroll to find them every time.
Hidden Conversations
The conversation panel with AI summaries is hidden behind a button. Most users never discover it.
No Status Indicators
Can't see which conversations need attention (hot) vs. which have gone quiet (stalled).
Flat Structure
All streams in one list. No grouping, no collapsible sections, no visual hierarchy.

Recommendations

Based on the explorations above, here's what I think would work best:

1. Enhanced Items with Previews
Show author, preview text, and relative time for every stream. This alone eliminates 80% of the "must open to see what's happening" problem.
2. Pinned Section
Let users pin their most important streams. Pinned items always visible at top, regardless of sort order.
3. Default to "Recent" Sort
Activity-based sorting as default. Most recent conversations float to top. Option to switch to alphabetical.
4. "Needs Attention" Section (Phase 2)
AI-powered detection of conversations waiting on the user. Questions, mentions, follow-ups. Surface them prominently.
